About me

Alongside problem-solving and optimization, I have hands-on experience in full-stack development using the MERN stack. I have also developed projects such as a Subscription Tracker & Optimizer, a Job Portal with admin CRUD operations, a CV Builder, and interactive movie applications featuring watchlists, modals, and rating systems. I also have experience integrating payment gateways like Stripe, handling token management, and optimizing application workflows. In addition to personal projects, I have gained exposure to real-world development practices such as debugging production issues, query optimization, understanding database-level workflows, solving N+1 query problems, and improving application performance. I continuously focus on writing clean, maintainable code, enhancing user experience, and strengthening my software engineering skills through hands-on development and problem-solving.
